fried rice

fried rice is also enjoyed by africans either at home,party or anywhere at all it is much appreciated .

ingredients
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 eggs
  • salt and pepper
instructions
  1. heat oil,add onion
  2. push onion aside,crack in eggs .scramble
  3. mix eggs with onion
  4. add cooked rice,salt,and pepper.stir-fry
