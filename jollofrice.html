jollof rice

jollof rice is an african dish that is enjoyed by thousands of africans .below is the recipe for it

ingredients
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp of tomato paste
  • salt and pepper
instructions
  1. heat oil ,add onion
  2. add tomato paste ,salt,and pepper
  3. add rice and water
  4. biol,then simmer for 20-25 minutes
  5. your jollof rice can be eaten with any protein of your chioce
