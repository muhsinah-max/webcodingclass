egusi soup

egusi soup is a nigerian delicacy usually taken with swallow or rice

ingredients
  • 1 cup ground egusi seeds
  • 1 pound meat (beef,goat,or fish)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cups water
  • salt and pepper
  • palm oil
  • bullion cups
instructions
  1. boil the meat and onion
  2. add ground egusi seeds and stir
  3. add water and bring to a boil
  4. reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes
  5. season with salt,pepper, and bullion cubes
  6. serve with fufu,eba,pounded yam (any swallow) or rice
