jollof rice

jollof rice is a spicy,flavorful West African one-pot dish made with rice,tomatoes and spices

ingredients
  1. 1 cup of rice
  2. 2 cups of water
  3. 2 tbsp oil
  4. 1 onion
  5. 1 tsp of tomato paste
  6. salt and pepper
    7. instructions
    1. heat oil ,add onion
    2. add tomato paste ,salt,and pepper
    3. add rice and water
    4. biol,then simmer for 20-25 minutes
    5. your jollof rice can be eaten with any protein of your chioce
